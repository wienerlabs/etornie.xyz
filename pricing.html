Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 15 days free of charge with full Firm-tier features. No card required to start.

What does “per counsel” mean?

Each licensed seat is one practicing attorney. Paralegals, support staff, and admins are included for free.

Are EtornieGPT API calls metered?

No. Predictable pricing — AI usage is included up to fair-use limits on every plan.

Do I pay extra for on-chain attestations?

Solana network fees are passed through at cost (typically < $0.01 per attestation). No platform markup.
